Banku And Tomato Pepper Sauce With Fried Fish

hot!!!

Hello everyone! GHANA TASTE Banku mix, with crispy whole black seabream and homemade meko (pepper sauce)! very simple and amazing!

Ingredients for Banku

  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for fish

  • Black sea bream
  • Fish seasoning
  • Adobo
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Hot chilli powder
  • Curry powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Deep frying oil
  • 1/4 onion
  • F1 tbsp flour

Ingredients for tomato pepper sauce

  1. 1/2 onion
  2. 1 scotch bonnet
  3. plum to matoes or frsh tomatoes
  4. salt
  5. crayfish seasoning
  6. extra onion
